Hi, my name is Thomas Blankers and I currently work as a Marie Curie post-doctoral fellow in Astrid Groot's lab at the University of Amsterdam. My work focusses on speciation genetics and covers a wide range of topics including the genetic architecture of behavioral isolation, population genetics, and functional genetics. Read more about my current and past research on Heliothine moths, Hawaiian and North American crickets, and global diversity of salamanders and lizards in the Research section of my website.
